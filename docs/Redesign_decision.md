***“Free Shipping for this item” below add to cart button***

“Free Shipping for this item” line under the add to cart button give users a nudge to add it to cart. Currently even though it’s free shipment they actively need to read the Free shipment block, undersatnd and qualify this item is free shipment by themselves. If the item is below 10000huf line text “Min. order value 10000huf for free shipment”

I assume the highlighted block of Free shipping for over 10000huf order is to increase upsell. But I think we can have that information in the checkout page somewhere close to the shipment fee that when they are actively thinking of shipping fee and can add additional products recommendation that takes the order value over the threshold. 

I think a single purpose page converts more, the sole purpose of a product page is that user add to cart the product not create friction of finding another product to qualify for free shipment it should be on the later step of buying the cycle.
